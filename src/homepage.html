Trending on travel

Trending today is the desire to have a personal connection in the destination they are visiting.

Trending today for a growing segment of the traveling public is attention to the social conscience and ethical considerations of travel. Todays traveler is interested in sustainable travel that minimizes the impact on local, culturural and physical environments in the world.

Trending in travel of all types is an emphasis on more active and wellness focused offerings. Clients want walking tours, spa treatments, Pilates and yoga classes, more biking, hiking, and kayaking opportunities. Trending now is the popularity of luxury in travel choices. Travelers want more all inclusive trips, they want exquisite cuisine, they choose suites in hotels and staterooms on board cruise lines. While they are choosing these things, they also choose adventure and experiential travel.
